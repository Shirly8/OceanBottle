Three ways we act

Every OceanBottle funds a chain of action — from policy to coastline.

01

Environmental lobbying

We push governments to protect oceans — improved recycling infrastructure, collector benefits, and traceability across the Americas, Africa, and Asia.

02

Coastal clean-ups

Funding goes directly to collection partners. Global teams organise clean-ups and plastic waste collections that clear litter around coastlines.

03

Partners send us verified collection data. We publish reports, fact sheets, and media on the real state of our ocean — turning data into global awareness.
